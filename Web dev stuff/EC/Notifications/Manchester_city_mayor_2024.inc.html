Election of mayor of the city of Manchester


Location: Manchester
Date: 23/10/2024
The candidates standing in this election are are follows:
Jack Stewart,TORY   Craig Russell,LABR   Ashley Gray,DEMS   Hannah Griffin,ENVI

The mayor of manchester will be elected on 23/10/24, the incumbant, Maud Howard(LABR) is set to step down from the role.

Please consult your local authority if you are unaware of the location of your polling station.
Please ensure that you are on the electoral register prior to voting. You will be unable to vote in this election if you are not registered by 11:59pm on 09/10/2024.

The ballot is open only to Residents of the city of manchester, check with your local authority if you are unsure of you eligibility.
