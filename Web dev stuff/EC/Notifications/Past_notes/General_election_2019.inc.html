The 2019 parliamentary general election is scheduled to take place on 12/12/2024, voting will take place in every constituency in the UK.
Information on each of the candidates, including policy ideas, political history and hustings times and locations can be found online.
Information including House of Commons addresses, vote history and expenses history relating to any MP can be found on the hansard website.

Please consult your local authority if you are unaware of the location of your polling station.
Please ensure that you are on the electoral register prior to voting. You will be unable to vote in this election if you are not registered by 11:59pm on 30/11/2024.

The ballot is open to all non-disenfranchised citizens of the UK, if you are an EU national or not a UK citizen you will be unable to take part.
