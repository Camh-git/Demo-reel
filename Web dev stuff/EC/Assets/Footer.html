The electoral commision is a sub-department of the UK home office responsible for monitering and verify elections.
The commision undertakes investigations into any potential breaches of electoral law and co-operates with all relevnt law enforcement agencies to do so.

This website uses minimal cookies and does not track you in any way, we respect your privacy, your rights remain unaffected.

To make an offical enquiry please contact us or the UK home office using the information provided or your own sources.
