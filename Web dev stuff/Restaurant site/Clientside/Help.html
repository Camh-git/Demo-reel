Ordering help

Here is a simple rundown of how to order: navigate to an item and click"add to cart", do this for every item you want, then go to your cart, either by pressing "add to cart" on an item or clickng the "basket" tab in the header, from there click checkout and follow the prompts.
You can also call us using the info on the "about us" page or pop in store if you prefer.
